### Podere La Chiusella B&B
**Podere Chiusella** , was an old farm, is now a wonderfull place where to spend holidays in to the Terre Senesi Area.
The apartments are all fully restructured and furnished with antique and hand-made furniture and furnishing complements.
Every apartment is provided with all modern comforts. There are also furnished external common areas: swimming pool, garden, courtyard, a wide parking area, covered and fenced, ping-pong and bicycles.
Satellite TV, safety box and independent heating. Wireless free.
Also, guests of Podere “La Chiusella” are offered a 10% discount on treatments at the health spa “Antica Querciolaia”.
